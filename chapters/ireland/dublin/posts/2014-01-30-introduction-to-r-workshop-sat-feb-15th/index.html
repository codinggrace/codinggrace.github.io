FAQ

What do I need to bring?

Bring your laptop with the following installed already before coming to the workshop:

  • Download and install the latest version of R via http://ftp.heanet.ie/mirrors/cran.r-project.org/bin/windows/base/
  • Download and install RStudio, choose the version that is under the heading “Recommended for your system” via http://www.rstudio.com/ide/download/desktop

I see it’s female-friendly, are guys welcome as well?

You are welcome as a +1. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me at codinggrace@gmail.com.
